Hello everyone,
As you hopefully know this week is LCC’s earth week and we are very happy to announce the activities that will take place the following days. Here’s the plan:

Monday

We want to encourage everyone to think about one way to live more sustainably and tell us about your pledges. For more details, refer to the e-mail you received yesterday.

Tuesday

Join us for a fun Kahoot and test your knowledge of the environment. To sign up, e-mail greenteam@wearelcc.ca.

Wednesday

We have prepared Earth Day videos that will be shown during assembly. In one video, we will be answering FAQs. Submit your questions about the environment here: bit.ly/LCC_Green_Team_FAQ_Environment

Thursday: 

Using Netflix Party you are welcome to join members of the Green Team to watch the documentary “There’s Something in the Water”, which will be followed by a discussion. E-mail greenteam@wearelcc.ca to sign up. Students, parents, teachers and faculty are invited!

Thank you for your participation and happy earth day,
The Green Team
