Earth Day!

I know, I know, you are all very excited. Excited for what? Well, Earth Day, of course! This year, Earth Day will be taking place Wednesday, April 22, which is just in time for the next assembly (there is no assembly this coming week)! Consequently, the Green Team is currently very busy preparing the exciting media that has been our standard since the beginning of the year. In fact, you may have already noticed that some of Green Team's respective committees have begun sending out information regarding the various activities they have in store for you.

One of such activities is a virtual FAQ. What is an FAQ? FAQ stands for Frequently Asked Questions. Usually, these pages, found on websites, are places users can consult in order to find answers to their questions (as is implied by the acronym). Essentially, we ask you to submit at least one (but you are welcome to submit more) question that you would like us to answer. This question can be anything, though we believe it would make more sense if it were related to the environment. We are the Green Team, after all!

Answer!

We will then answer these questions during a virtual Q&A session which will be presented on Earth Day (provided there is enough participation)! We encourage everyone to ask entertaining questions, though please remember to use your judgement and keep in mind that there are things we cannot say.

Looking Forward

We look forward to your submissions and once again, remind you that more information has yet to come. Stay tuned and remember to stay green!
